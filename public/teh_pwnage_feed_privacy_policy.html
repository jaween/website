Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 4, 2025

Teh Pwnage Feed is a simple Atom feed viewer designed to display content from a single, specific feed. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to keeping things as private and minimal as possible.

What We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not track your activity or usage patterns. There are no analytics or background data collection mechanisms.

Feed Content

The app only fetches content from a publicly available Atom feed and displays it within the app interface. All content comes from that external source and is shown as-is.

External Links

When you click on a link within the feed, it will open in your device’s default browser or external application. Teh Pwnage Feed does not intercept, modify, or monitor these links.

Data Storage

No data is stored on your device beyond what is needed to display the feed content temporarily while the app is running. Once the app is closed, this data is discarded.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be made available here with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out to the app developer.
